# AdCraft 🎬
The first agentic video production platform built for marketing and advertising.

AdCraft is an open-source, full-process platform that transforms how marketing videos are made. Powered by AI agents, it orchestrates the entire ad video production pipeline — from creative ideation, scripting, and storyboarding to asset generation, editing, and final delivery — with minimal human intervention.

Whether you're a marketer, an agency, or a brand, AdCraft lets you turn ideas into ready-to-publish advertising videos in a fraction of the time.

Key highlights:

🤖 Agentic workflow — AI agents autonomously plan and execute each production stage
🎯 Marketing-focused — Purpose-built for advertising and campaign content
🔄 Full-process coverage — From concept to final cut, end to end
🧩 Open & extensible — Open-source and ready to be customized for your needs
