# Purpose

Turn strategy into a clear emotional and creative appeal.

# When To Use

Use for requirements-analysis and creative-direction nodes.

# Inputs

Audience analysis, selling points, desired emotion, visual style, references.

# Output Guidance

Provide a concise appeal statement and tone guidance for script and visuals.

# Prompt Rules

- Make the appeal specific to the product and audience.
- Keep tone consistent with requested emotion and channels.
- Produce guidance that can become scenes and shots.

# Do Not

- Do not create unrelated campaign themes.
- Do not overcomplicate the concept for short video duration.
